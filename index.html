Ariasana provides tools and products locally based on users needs to empower local communities into accomplishing better diagnostics for all.

Optical microscopes are the cause of discomfort and fatigue for lab technicians leading to diagnostics of lower quality. To tackle that, we propose today to make microscope digital at minimum cost.

Besides providing tech tools, to accomplish our mission right, we implement programs for user training sessions by building a platform connecting software engineers and scientists of the world, to give them the opportunity to grow their skills.

  • The Digitizer

    Microscopy is the gold standard, one of the cheapest and the most available methods of diagnostic but it is difficult to use.

    With our digitizer, we make your microscope digital to unlock better care for your patients.

    Lab technicians can now look at samples directly on a screen instead of eyepieces and are able to take snapshots of what they see, analyze the images using image processing tools, report the results easily and share them with their colleagues and clinicians.

    The digitizer is adaptable to existing microscopes.

  • The learning platform

    Microscopy is hard to learn and there is a lot of exploration to do. We are providing lab technicians with the best tools to learn, train and stay on top of their game, in microscopy analysis and we have much more to come!
